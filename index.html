ABOUT DEADLINING

As neighborhoods transform, Black women become both targets and warnings.

Their disappearances and murders occur within fragmented systems of reporting, investigation, jurisdiction, and public memory.

THE FINDING

Documented cases cluster within liminal zones of urban redevelopment and jurisdictional invisibility.

GEOGRAPHY OF INVISIBILITY

The highest documented case density occurred along Suitland Parkway, where redevelopment is transforming a corridor long shaped by displacement.

WHY THIS DATABASE EXISTS

To close critical gaps in missing and murdered persons data through a publicly accessible forensic database supporting investigation, research, and informed public awareness.
