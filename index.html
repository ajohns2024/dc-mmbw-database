About “DEADLINING”:

As neighborhoods transform, Black female bodies become both targets and warnings,

Their murders are simultaneously rising in number yet falling in priority.

Geography of Invisibility:

The Finding:

Documented cases cluster within liminal zones of urban "Redevelopment" & jurisdictional invisibility.

WHY WE EXIST: To close critical gaps in missing and murdered persons data by creating a publicly accessible forensic database that supports investigations and advances geospatial research into the structural conditions that shape victimization.
