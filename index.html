RESEARCH INITIATIVE

ABOUT DEADLINING

Deadlining investigates how missing and murdered Black women become obscured within systems of urban redevelopment, jurisdiction, reporting, and public memory.

01

THE FINDING

Documented cases cluster within liminal zones of urban redevelopment and jurisdictional invisibility.

02

GEOGRAPHY OF INVISIBILITY

The highest documented case density occurred along Suitland Parkway, where redevelopment is transforming a corridor long shaped by displacement.

03

WHY WE EXIST

To close critical gaps in missing and murdered persons data through an accessible forensic database supporting investigation, research, and informed public awareness.
